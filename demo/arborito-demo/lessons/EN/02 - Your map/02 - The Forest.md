@section
index: 1
title: The Forest
@/section

Open **Forest** from the main menu. It's the **filing cabinet** for your branches: what you open, import, or plant new.

@section
index: 2
title: Open and import branches
@/section

From the Forest you can:

- **Open** a branch you already have (like this demo).
- **Import** a `.arborito` file someone sent you.
- **Plant a new branch** if you want to write your own from scratch.

Branches **you** open or create rise to the top of the list. This demo stays handy as a built-in guide.

@section
index: 3
title: Progress and device
@/section

By default your progress is saved **on this device**. Switching phones or clearing browser data can reset points and streak; the **content** of your branches stays in the Forest if you didn't delete them.

Exporting from construction builds a `.arborito` with folders and lessons; whoever imports it starts their own progress.

@section
index: 4
title: Read-only and editable copy
@/section

Some Forest branches are **read-only** (like this demo or public courses from another author). To edit them for real, use **Copy to My garden** in construction: you get a copy under another name that you can save.

That way you explore someone else's content without breaking the original.
