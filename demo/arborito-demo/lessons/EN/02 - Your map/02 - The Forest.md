# The Forest

Open **Forest** from the main menu. It is the **filing cabinet** for your branches: what you open, import, or plant anew.

@section
title: Open and import branches
@/section

From the Forest you can:

- **Open** a branch you already have (like this demo).
- **Import** a `.arborito` file someone sent you.
- **Plant new branch** if you want to start your own from scratch.

Branches you **open or create** rise to the top of the list. This demo stays available until you delete it or favor another branch.

@section
title: Progress and device
@/section

By default your progress is saved **on this device**. Switching phones or clearing browser data may reset points and streak; **content** stays in the Forest unless you deleted the branch.

Exporting from construction builds a `.arborito` with folders and lessons; whoever imports it starts their own progress.

@section
title: Online branches and editable copy
@/section

Some Forest branches are **read-only** (like this demo or public courses from another author). To edit them for real use **Copy to My garden** in construction: you get a copy with another name you can save.

Explore others' content without breaking the original.
