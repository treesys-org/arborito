@section
index: 2
title: Open folders and lessons
@/section

- **Tap a folder:** it opens and you see the lessons inside.
- **Tap a lesson:** the reading panel opens on the right (or below on mobile).
- **Go back:** arrow or breadcrumbs at the top.

The panel keeps text, images, and questions in one place. You don't need to jump between apps.

@section
index: 3
title: Lesson panel and outline
@/section

To the left of the text (or above on mobile) you'll see the **outline**: sections and quizzes. Each row is a step. You must **pass** quizzes to mark them green.

That way you know what still needs review. You can jump section to section with the outline.

@section
index: 4
title: Mobile and useful shortcuts
@/section

On a small screen the map stays center stage: the lesson panel can take the full height. Main menus (Forest, Progress, settings) live in the bottom or side bar depending on the device.

Look for the **Play** 🎮 button when a folder is open: that's how you enter Arcade with the current course.
