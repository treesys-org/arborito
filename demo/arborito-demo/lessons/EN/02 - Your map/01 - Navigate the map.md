# Your map

The **map** is the main screen with circles and folders. It is your desk: from here you open the whole course without getting lost.

@section
title: Open folders and lessons
@/section

- **Tap a folder** → it opens and you see lessons inside.
- **Tap a lesson** → the reading panel opens on the right (or below on mobile).
- **Go back** → arrow or breadcrumbs at the top.

The panel keeps text, images, and questions in one place. No jumping between apps.

@section
title: Lesson panel and outline
@/section

On the left of the text (or above on mobile) you will see the **outline**: `@section` blocks and `@quiz` blocks. Each row is a step. You must **pass** quizzes to mark them green.

That way you know what to review without guessing. Jump between sections using the outline.

@section
title: Mobile and useful shortcuts
@/section

On a small screen the map stays central: the lesson panel may take full height. Main menus (Forest, Progress, settings) live in the bottom or side bar depending on device.

Look for the **Play** 🎮 button when a folder is open: that is how you enter Arcade with the current course.
