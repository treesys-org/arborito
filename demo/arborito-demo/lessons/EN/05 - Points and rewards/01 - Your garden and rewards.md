@section
index: 1
title: Points and rewards
@/section

Arborito **gamifies** your study so it's easier to come back each day. It isn't a competitive video game: it's **your personal progress**.

@image
url: ./media/11-jardin-en.png
caption: Your memory garden: seeds and progress you see as you study.
@/image

@section
index: 2
title: XP, streak, and garden
@/section

- **Points (XP / lumens)** when you finish lessons and pass questions.
- **Streak** if you study several days in a row.
- **Leaf shield (umbrella):** every 3 streak days you earn one (max 3). If you miss a day, it spends a shield and **keeps your streak**. You can also buy more in the **Garden shop** (backpack / Progress).
- **Seeds** in your garden when you finish modules (see **Progress** or the backpack).

When you earn or use a shield, Arborito shows a short toast and a brief celebration flash.

Each outline quiz adds a sense of progress: that's why this demo's lessons carry several questions per block.

@section
index: 3
title: Branch and tree trophies
@/section

In the backpack **Achievements** you'll see three kinds:

1. **Branch:** you finish **an entire** Forest branch (like this demo).
2. **Tree:** you finish **every** branch in a tree (several courses grouped together).
3. **Optional achievement:** the author turns it on for a specific map folder (construction, folder menu, Enable achievement).

Branch and tree trophies count **even if you're inside a map folder**.

@section
index: 4
title: Motivation without pressure
@/section

Points and trophies are there to **celebrate**, not to punish. You can study at your pace, retry quizzes, and play Arcade without losing what you learned.
