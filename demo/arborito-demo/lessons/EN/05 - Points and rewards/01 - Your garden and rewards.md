# Points and rewards

Arborito **gamifies** study so coming back each day feels easier. It is not a competitive video game: it is **your personal progress**.

@section
title: XP, streak, and garden
@/section

- **Points (XP)** when you complete lessons and pass questions.
- **Streak** when you study several days in a row.
- **Seeds** in your garden when you finish modules (see **Progress** / backpack 🎒).

Each outline quiz adds a sense of momentum: that is why demo lessons pack several `items` per block.

@section
title: Branch and tree trophies
@/section

In the backpack **Achievements** you will see three kinds:

1. **Branch:** finish **every** lesson in a Forest branch (like this demo).
2. **Tree:** finish **all** branches in a tree (playlist with several branches).
3. **Optional achievement:** author enables 🏆 on a specific map folder (construction → ⋮ menu → Enable achievement).

Branch and tree trophies count **even when you are inside a map folder**.
