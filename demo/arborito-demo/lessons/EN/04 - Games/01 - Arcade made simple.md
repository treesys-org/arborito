@section
index: 1
title: Arcade: play what you study
@/section

**Arcade** is mini-games inside Arborito. **They aren't separate apps**: they read the **questions from your lessons** and turn them into play sessions.

@image
url:./media/07-arcade-en.png
caption: Arcade: pick a game and play with the course you have open.
@/image

@section
index: 2
title: Enter Arcade
@/section

1. Open a **folder** or branch on the map.
2. Find the **Play** / **Arcade** button (controller 🎮).
3. Pick a game: Classroom, Memory, Hacky Terminal, Alonso Duel…

If you don't see the button, open a folder with lessons first. Arcade uses the course you have open.

@section
index: 3
title: Static and dynamic mode
@/section

**Static mode** uses only course content: it works without AI and is perfect for reviewing on the train.

**Dynamic mode** (with AI on) lets **Sage** generate variations or help in some games. You choose in settings.

@section
index: 4
title: Games in this demo
@/section

Some you'll see in Arcade:

- **Classroom**: a class with fictional classmates.
- **Memory**: concept/answer pairs (also with your memory garden).
- **Hacky Terminal**: a retro console for review.
- **Alonso Duel**: a fast duel with the same questions.

All of them read the branch you're exploring. The better the lessons, the richer Arcade feels.
