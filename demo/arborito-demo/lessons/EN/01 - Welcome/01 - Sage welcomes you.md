# Hi, I'm Sage

I'm **Sage**. I wrote this demo branch so you can learn Arborito **without weird manuals**: map, Forest, lessons, Arcade, and construction, in the order I'd use with a new student.

@section
title: Who I am and what Arborito does
@/section

Arborito is a **study notebook with a map**. Open folders, read, practice with built-in questions, and if you feel like it, play mini-games with the same material you just read.

I can answer questions if you turn on AI in settings. It is **off** by default: you choose when to talk to me.

@section
title: Branch, lesson, and trophy
@/section

1. **Branch** = one full course (like this "Arborito demo"). Finish it and you earn a **branch trophy** 🏆.
2. **Lesson** = one page with text, images, and questions in the side outline.
3. **Tree** = several branches together (playlist). Finish all for a tree trophy.

> **Video:** if the author links a YouTube walkthrough, you will see it in the lesson. Until then, this demo **is** the step-by-step tour.

@section
title: How to follow this demo
@/section

Walk the map folders in order: **Your map**, **Lessons**, **Games**, **Points**, **Construction**, and the **Final exam**. Each lesson has an **outline** on the left: sections and quizzes. Green ticks show what you already nailed.

If something feels off, go back on the map or repeat a quiz: in normal lessons you can practice again without losing the green tick.
