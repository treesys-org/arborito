@section
index: 2
title: Text and sections
@/section

Lessons are split into **sections** so the outline has clear steps. Read at your pace, use the outline to jump, and go back when you need a review.

Images and other media sit between sections with the rest of the content.

@section
index: 3
title: Quizzes with several questions
@/section

One quiz can hold **several questions in a row**. In the outline it counts as **one** step; inside you practice several ideas without fragmenting the lesson.

Each question can use different modes (multiple choice, recall, cloze…). The "Five question types" lesson shows them one by one on purpose.

@section
index: 4
title: From lesson to Arcade
@/section

The same questions you practice here feed **Classroom**, **Memory**, **Hacky Terminal**, and other games. Study once, play many times: the author doesn't duplicate work.

That's why this demo mixes clear text with multi-question quizzes: it's the standard you'd see in a published course.
